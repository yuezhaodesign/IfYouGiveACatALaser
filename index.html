If You Give a Cat a Laser

Scene One

A small, red dot appears, flickering near the wall. The cat's ears perk up, eyes sharpening as it spots the light.

Cat: (whispering in awe) "Oh! What's that? Could it be...a signal from…an alien spaceship?"

The red dot dances across the floor, taunting the cat. The cat’s pupils dilate as it hunkers down, ready to pounce.

Cat: (scrambling after it, breathless) "Wait! Don't run! Don't run away!"

The dot zigzags and swerves, leading the cat on a wild chase, bouncing from wall to wall, and skittering across the floor.

Cat: (desperately, paws stretching toward the dot) "Take me! Take me! I want to leave this planet!"

Scene Two

A fluffy CAT is lounging on a cozy blanket, looking aloof. A HUMAN HAND slowly enters the frame, reaching to pet the cat.

Human Hand: (gently pets the cat)

Cat: (tensing up, fur bristling) "Don't touch me!!"(hisses and swats at the hand)

"Meow! Meow! Meow!"

The human hand quickly retracts, then reappears holding a small, colorful MOUSE TOY. The hand offers it to the cat.

Cat: (suddenly calm, eyeing the toy approvingly) "Much better."

(begins to paw at the toy, batting it around with interest)

The human hand slowly withdraws as the cat happily plays with the mouse toy.
